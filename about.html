About ADE ZIRI ENCGO

The ENCGO Student Association, also known as ADE ZIRI, is a dynamic organization that serves as the voice of students at the National School of Business and Management of Oujda (ENCGO). We are committed to enhancing student life through both academic and extracurricular initiatives.

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to develop their leadership skills, network with professionals, and participate in various social, cultural, and sporting activities. We believe in the power of collaboration and strive to create a sense of community within the student body.

  • Empower students through diverse events and workshops
  • Promote academic excellence and innovation
  • Foster teamwork and leadership
  • Connect students with local and international opportunities
